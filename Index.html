Habitat Diet Fun Facts

Cat Habitat

Domestic cats live in homes, farms, and cities if they have shelter, food, and water. Wild cats live in places like grasslands, forests, and mountains depending on the species

What Cats Eat

Cats are carnivores and mainly eat meat. Domestic cats eat cat food like meat and fish, while wild cats hunt animals such as birds, rodents, and deer.

Fun Facts

  1. Cats can rotate their ears 180° to hear sounds from all directions.
  2. Cats sleep 12–16 hours a day, spending most of their life napping.
  3. A group of cats is called a clowder, and a baby cat is a kitten.

Most common Cat breed

Persian – Calm, gentle cats with long, fluffy coats. Perfect for cozy homes.

Siamese – Slim cats with blue eyes, very talkative and playful.

Maine Coon – Big, friendly cats often called gentle giants.

Bengal – Spotted like leopards, full of energy and adventure.

Sphynx – Hairless, affectionate cats that love warmth and cuddles.
